Technical Events

1. 404 FINDER (Quiz)

➼ 2 members per team.
➼ 2 rounds will be conducted.
➼ Questions will be based on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Python, SQL.
➼ Game will be conducted through kahoot websites.
➼ Participants are requested to bring the laptops or mobile phones.

2. Simplify To Amplify (DashBoard Creation)

➼ 2 members per team.
➼ Required Skill: PowerBI, Tableau.
➼ In Prelims, Datasets and Dashboards will be provided, Participants are asked to explain the content of the dashboard.
➼ In Finals the participants are asked to create an innovative dashboard provided the datasets. PowerBi accounts will be provided.
➼ Systems will be Provided.

3. Zoom In Zoom Out (Identification & Code Reducing)

➼ 2 members per team.
➼ Time span will be provided on the spot.
➼ Required skill: Python, Jupyter(IDE).
➼ Participants are ask to identify and reduce the lines of code that was given.
➼ Mobile phones and other electronic gadgets are prohibited.

4. Sketch AI (Image Creation)

➼ 2 members per team.
➼ Picture will be given on the Spot.
➼ Enhance the picture using any known programming language.
➼ System with internet will be provided.
➼ Internet facilities are Available.
